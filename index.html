About Our Church

Founded in early 2025, Barrhaven Bible Chapel is a new assembly of believers dedicated to serving God and our community.

As servants of Christ and stewards of the mysteries of God, we are committed to preaching about God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit. Our mission is to share God's love and His restoration plan with those who are earnestly seeking Him, while teaching the whole counsel of God to those who have believed in the Lord Jesus Christ as their personal Saviour and Lord.

Our Vision

To be a place where God's purpose for man is fulfilled through the work of the Holy Spirit.

Our Community

A gathering of believers united in faith, seeking to live God's life in our mortal bodies.

Our Values

Preaching God's love and teaching His purposes, not promoting religion but fostering true spiritual growth.
